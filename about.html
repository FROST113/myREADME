About Me

I am Software Developer with three years work experience. I started my coding journey back in 2019. I did online courses learning the fundamentals of Frontend Development. While I was busy with my courses I interned at a small Development company aiding in creating websites. Mainly creating the layout and styling the UI. This aided me in learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript in more of a practical way by applying it to a real life projects for clients.

With more experience under my belt I started working more with the Angular Framework and getting use to the component based method to create mobile responsive UI. I started to focus more on the User Experience (UX) aspect for better looking and feeling software. My responsibilities grew as I became a full time employee at the company I interned at and working more closely with my seniors aiding with the connections of end points from the backend and creating more complex UI designs.

At that point I started looking more into how the backend of a website/system worked and how to make that connection with the backend. The fist backend language I started looking into was C#. I still have much to learn but that set me down the path to become a Full Stack developer one day.
